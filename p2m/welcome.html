The System
7 phases from idea to income.
Each phase has specific steps, checklists, and a clear output. Complete them in order. Go/No-Go gates prevent bad ideas from wasting your time and money.
Phase 1 — Day 1

Ideation & Validation

Define the product, research the market, score the opportunity, and do the income math. Pass the Go/No-Go gate before spending a dime on design.

Product Idea Market Research Competitive Analysis Niche Score Income Math GO / NO-GO
Phase 2 — Days 1-3

Design & Engineering

CAD model, prototype iterations, and locked print settings. You don't move forward until the physical product meets your quality bar.

CAD Design Prototype & Iterate Lock Print Settings
Phase 3 — Days 3-4

Cost Model & Pricing

True COGS calculation, FBA fee stacking, margin validation, and price positioning. Know your numbers before you list.

COGS Breakdown Pricing Strategy Variation Pricing Break-even
Phase 4 — Days 4-5

Packaging & Fulfillment

Packaging design, UPC/FNSKU setup, and FBA shipment prep. Get shelf-ready and warehouse-ready at the same time.

Packaging Design FBA Compliance Batch Workflow
Phase 5 — Days 5-7

Listing & Launch

Barcodes, keyword-optimized listing, product photography, variations, and launch checklist. A Go/No-Go gate confirms you're live and discoverable.

Barcodes/UPC Listing Content Photography Variations Launch Checklist GO / NO-GO
Phase 6 — Days 7-30+

Marketing & Growth

PPC campaigns, review strategy, social media, and organic ranking. This is where consistent effort compounds into sales.

What's Inside
Every tool a maker-seller needs.
P2M isn't just a checklist. It's a data-driven system with layered detail behind every decision.
■

Visual Pipeline

See every product's progress at a glance. Color-coded phases, LED status indicators, completion bars, and Mark as Complete buttons with undo.

⚙

Workshop Setup

Configure your entire production workshop: fleet of printers, materials library, machine economics, labor, and software. Workshop data flows automatically into every product's Pipeline.

🔗

Cross-Page Data Bridge

Workshop and Pipeline talk to each other. Your blended rate, material costs, and fleet data auto-populate COGS calculations — no re-entering data.

☰

Layered Data Model

Every step has a Summary (quick-glance fields), Analysis (conclusions from data), and Evidence (raw supporting data). Nothing is a guess.

☑

Step Checklists

Actionable checklists for every step ensure nothing gets skipped. Check them off as you go — your progress persists across browser sessions.

✎

Multi-Product Support

Run multiple products through the pipeline simultaneously. Each product has its own LED states, data, and checklists — completely isolated.
